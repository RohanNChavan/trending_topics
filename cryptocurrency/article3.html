A. Top Crypto Trading Platforms: A Global Perspective

United States

Coinbase

Founded in 2012, Coinbase has established itself as a trusted name in crypto trading with a market cap of ~$35 billion. Known for its regulatory compliance and user-friendly interface, it's often the first choice for beginners.

Kraken

Established in 2011, Kraken focuses on security and advanced trading features. With a market cap of ~$20 billion, it's known for its robust security measures and proof of reserves system.

European Union

Binance

With a market cap of ~$60 billion, Binance dominates the global crypto market. Known for its extensive crypto offerings and Binance Smart Chain ecosystem.
